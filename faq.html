HackBMU 2.O

FAQ

  1. When and Where?

    HackBMU '19 will take place from February 9th to 10th at BML Munjal University in Gurgaon, Haryana in the hall NB-105.

  2. Not a student?

    Become a mentor! Give back to the community by helping hackers learn and build something awesome.

  3. What can I build?

    Whatever you can think up! We’ll have mentors to help you with web, mobile, hardware, design, and any development in between!

  4. How does registration work?

    After you apply, you will receive a confirmation email. Later, you'll get an acceptance email and you can RSVP to reserve your spot.

  5. How long is it?

    You will have 30 hours to build but we will also have an opening and closing ceremonies as well as the project expo. Prepare to spend your weekend with us starting Saturday afternoon and we’ll have you out around evening on Sunday. A more detailed scheduled will be released closer to the event.

  6. Food?

    Food. Enough free grub to keep you hacking for 24 hours. Dietary restrictions? No problem, we got you.

  7. What if this is my first hackathon?

    Awesome! We have prizes for newcomers, as the best way to learn is by building. There will be mentors to help you at every step of the way. We all start somewhere, why not here?

  8. Am I invited?

    Anyone that is currently a college student or that has graduated in the past 2 year is invited. High schoolers can go ahead and apply and we'll notify you if any problems come about.

  9. How do teams work?

    Teams have a maximum of 4. If you don’t have a team, no sweat, we will have a mixer for you to discuss ideas and meet other hackers to join forces.

  10. Can I start working on my hack before the event?

    No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before the hackathon begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!

  11. What should I bring?

    Bring your student ID, laptop, chargers, a change of clothes, your resume, and toiletries. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well. You do not have to bring a team, idea, food, or any drinks. Please leave anything illegal at home.

  12. How to reach BML Munjal University?

    The closest metro station is IFFCO Chowk. You will be provided with free shuttle services from Iffco Metro to the venue.In case you want to travel alone you can get a bus which runs from IFFCO to Dharuhera or Bhiwadi till the stop BML Munjal University. It lies on the 67th km Milestone and is shortly after Mannat Dhaba and Fortune Park Orange.
